IV · Flow · ADR

Theme (quick cue):
Capturing decisions, taming chaos

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) capture context, options, decisions, and consequences for every significant technical choice. Embedding ADR writing in the development cadence preserves architectural intent, shortens onboarding, and halts endless rediscovery debates. Each concise, linkable document forms part of a living engineering journal that evolves alongside code, remaining searchable and reviewable. When assumptions shift, revisiting ADRs flags design drift early, guiding timely refactoring before small mismatches snowball into systemic risk. By externalising rationale, ADRs satisfy auditors and future maintainers alike, converting tribal knowledge into durable organisational memory.

IX The ADR — IX The ADR magnifies this minor’s impulse: it urges the architect to foreground clarity over chaos, recording not only what was chosen but why rejected paths were declined. When both appear, they spotlight missing rationale that threatens alignment and invite disciplined record-keeping.

XX The Documentation — XX The Documentation complements ADR’s snapshot by ensuring decisions remain discoverable amid sprawling knowledge bases. It asks whether your ADR index is searchable, versioned, and woven into onboarding material, turning isolated decision files into a coherent map for every new contributor.
