IV · Risk · Alert Fatigue

Theme (quick cue):
Paging noise, signal-to-noise, numb responders

When every minor hiccup pages the team, people quickly tune out. Alert Fatigue corrodes trust in monitoring so real fires burn longer. Sustainable architecture keeps signals meaningful: tie alerts to clear SLO breaches, suppress flapping metrics, and route low-urgency events to dashboards. Include context, severity, and one-click run-books so responders know what matters. Review paging data after every incident, retire noisy rules, rotate on-call, and share load. Protecting cognitive bandwidth preserves uptime, morale, and customer faith. Automated canary tests and anomaly detection further cut false pages, while error budgets create room for innovation without drowning operators in red.

The Outage — When Alert Fatigue reigns, The Outage lurks in silence. This fiery major warns that a single missed alarm can topple months of stability. Pair it with renewed pager drills and chaos testing, proving your system still screams loudly enough when real danger arrives.

The Governance — The Governance enters as cool air, setting boundaries that tame the siren chorus. It nudges architects to codify severity matrices, ownership tags, and escalation paths, turning raw noise into actionable insight and reminding teams that discipline, not heroics, sustains trustworthy service.
