V · Structure · Layering

Theme (quick cue):
Separation of concerns

Layering places each responsibility in its proper stratum so information flows downward and policy bubbles upward without shortcut tunnels. Presentation speaks to application services, which consult domain logic, which delegate persistence to infrastructure. Clear entry points let you swap databases, refactor UI frameworks, or introduce new interfaces without open-heart surgery on the core. Explicit boundaries shrink cognitive load, enable parallel work, and provide natural seams for automated testing, security controls, and deployment pipelines. When a layer breaks its contract, ripple effects are obvious and repairable. Good layering curbs accidental coupling while still allowing just-enough communication through well-designed ports and adapters.

The Architect — With Layering, The Architect surveys the elevation plan, ensuring each tier expresses a single concern and no beam bears hidden weight. Vision and balance turn the stack into a cathedral, not a tower of patch cords, guiding newcomers up orderly flights of abstraction.

The Governance — Layer boundaries are the guardrails of The Governance, converting policy into code by forbidding sideways calls and backdoor data leaks. Standards for crossing layers—DTOs, APIs, events—make compliance auditable and change approvals quick, proving that freedom thrives within clear, enforceable borders.
